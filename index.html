What is Stoky?

Stoky is a mobile and web app used by store owners to keep track of their inventory, cash and sales. A simple, yet powerful, interface streamlines transaction recording and makes all meaningful indicators just a clic away.

Try it free

Why retailers choose Stoky?

  • Accurate
  • Fast
  • Easy to use
  • Secure
  • Reliable
  • Offline

Accurate

Stoky is as accurate as a banking system. Every value is backed by a series of verifiable transactions. Stoky users can verify the accuracy of every stock value by drilling down to the transaction level.

Fast

Recording transactions on Stoky is really fast! We made sure that every transaction is accessible with a minimum of key presses.

Easy to use

We made an interface that speaks for itself, so you never have to read a user guide. Just take a minute or two to read through the menu and you will know what to do next.

Secure

Stoky is secured by state of the art technology made and managed by Google, so that you don’t have to worry about security, ever.

Reliable

Stoky runs inside Google's data centers. An infrastructure distributed all over the world. Not even a natural disaster can bring Stoky down.

Offline

Network interruptions will not interrupt your operations. Keep working offline, when you get back online, synchronization is done seamlessly.

Features

Features for end to end store management

Cash & Inventory

Stoky exists to secure cash and inventory. Every transaction recorded on Stoky will instantly update the cash and inventory levels. So you always know where your money and your inventory are.

Reporting

With one simple chart of daily sales and profits, you know exactly how your business performed over any given period.

Multi-Store

Running multiple stores? no problem. Just create as many stores and warehouses as you need and start transferring inventory between them.

Multi-User

Invite your team as sellers or managers to any of your stores at no extra cost.

Frequently asked questions

How can I switch from my current POS?

In your current POS, export your products to a spreadsheet. If export is not supported, copy paste your product list from a web interface into a spreadsheet. Reformat your spreadsheet to fit Stoky import format and import your products. Voila, your are ready to start selling.

Is Stoky really free?

Stoky is 100% free for businesses selling less than 10K $ per month. If you are selling more, take a look at your pricing page.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you downgrade to a plan with less sales volume than you currently have, all your transactions will still be accessible but you wont be able to record new ones.

What payment methods do you offer?

We support all major credit card payments and paypal via our payment partner Paddle.

What is your refund policy?

30 days money back guarantee, no questions asked.
