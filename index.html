What is wyzz?

Wyzz is a mobile and web app used by store owners to keep track of their inventory, cash and sales. A simple, yet powerful, interface streamlines transaction recording and makes all meaningful indicators just a clic away.

Open an account today

Why retailers choose wyzz?

Accurate

wyzz is as accurate as a banking system. Every value is backed by a series of verifiable transactions. wyzz users can verify the accuracy of every stock value by drilling down to the transaction level.

Fast

Recording transactions on wyzz is really fast! We made sure that every transaction is accessible with a minimum number of key presses.

Easy

We made an interface that speaks for itself, so you never have to read a user guide. Just take a minute or two to read through the menu and you will know what to do next.

Secure

wyzz is secured by state of the art technology made and managed by Google, so that you donâ€™t have to worry about security, ever.

Reliable

wyzz runs inside Google's data centers. An infrastructure distributed all over the world. Not even a natural disaster can bring wyzz down.

Offline

Network interruptions will not interrupt your operations. Keep working offline, when you get back online, synchronization is done seamlessly.

Features

Cash & Inventory

wyzz exists to secure cash and inventory. Every transaction recorded on wyzz will instantly update the cash and inventory levels. So you always know where your money and your inventory are.

Reporting

With one simple chart of daily sales and profits, you know exactly how your business performed over a given period.

Multi-Store

Running multiple stores? no problem. Just create as many stores and warehouses as you need and start transferring inventory between them.

Multi-User

Invite your team as sellers or managers to any of your stores at no extra cost.

How can I switch from my current POS?

From your current POS, export your products to a spreadsheet. If export is not supported, tcopy paste your product list from a web interface into a spreadsheet. Reformat your spreadsheet to fit Wyzz import format and import your products. Voila, your are ready to start selling.

Is Wyzz really free?

Wyzz is 100% free for businesses selling less than 10K $ per month. If you are selling more, take a look at your pricing page.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you downgrade to a plan with less sales volume than you currently have, all your transactions will still be accessible but you wont be able to record new ones.
