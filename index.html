What is Stoky?

Stoky is a mobile and web app used by store owners to keep track of their inventory, cash and sales. A simple, yet powerful, interface streamlines transaction recording and makes all meaningful indicators just a clic away.

Frequently asked questions

How can I switch from my current POS?

In your current POS, export your products to a spreadsheet. If export is not supported, copy paste your product list from a web interface into a spreadsheet. Reformat your spreadsheet to fit Stoky import format and import your products. Voila, your are ready to start selling.

Is Stoky really free?

Stoky is 100% free for businesses selling less than 10K $ per month. If you are selling more, take a look at your pricing page.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If you downgrade to a plan with less sales volume than you currently have, all your transactions will still be accessible but you wont be able to record new ones.

What payment methods do you offer?
